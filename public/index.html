Given for one instant an intelligence which could comprehend all the forces by which nature is animated and the respective situation of the beings who compose it (...), for it, nothing would be uncertain and the future, as the past, would be present to its eyes.

Pierre-Simon Laplace, A Philosophical Essay on Probabilities

This idea of a completely deterministic universe which strictly follows some laws of nature is what became known as

Laplace's Demon

and although the ideal of Laplace's Demon is unattainable, we can still strive for an ever-deeper understanding of the laws of nature. I hope to add my part to this endevaour by publishing my future projects and research on this website. My current interests lie at the intersection of Mathematics, Physics, and Artificial Intelligence.
